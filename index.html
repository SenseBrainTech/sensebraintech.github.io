About Us

Hi! We are SenseBrain Technology

We work closely with major image sensor manufacturer and smartphone OEMs on AI camera system integration. We provide turn-key solutions for all your computational photography need on your state-of-the-art mobile devices. Our R&D capabilities spans customized ISP designs for novel 2D image sensor, iToF and dToF 3D imaging, and system-level joint optics-electronics-algorithm optimization.

Optical Artifacts Removal

Our deep learning based optics engine works in conjunctions with physical optics model. It can remove complex optical artifacts introduced by the micro-structure of the optical elements and the sensor, including display pixel obstructions of under-display camera and lens imperfections due to manufacturing protocols.

AI-powered Pre-ISP Pipeline

Our demosaicing algorithms leverage both deep learning and image formation model to demosaic novel color filter arrays (CFAs), such as RGBW and 2x2 OCL quad-Bayer patterns, beyond the conventional Bayer pattern. Our solutions provide improved contrast and color performance compared to state-of-the-art results from our competitors.

Multi-frame Image Enhancement

Our multi-frame AI image enhancement solutions compensates for camera and object motions, reduces noise and reveals more details than state-of-the-art baselines.
